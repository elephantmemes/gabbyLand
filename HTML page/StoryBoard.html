THEME

My website using abstract backgrounds that have the colours; purple, blue and pink contrasted throughout the various webpages. The reason for choosing these colours and also images for my website was to allow the user to feel calm and relaxed when browsing through the pages.

I wanted to invokie this feeling to the users because my personality is one that is calm, energetic and also creative just like my website. Therefore the mix of these colours helped to potray my character but in the form of a website.
